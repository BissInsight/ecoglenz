Green Rhino® Lemon Dishwash Detergent uses biodegradable surfactants used for handwashing dishes, glasses, utensils, cutlery, pots and pans.

Download SDS

Green Rhino® Lemon Dishwash Detergent is a highly effective, eco-friendly solution for hand washing dishes, glassware, and cutlery. This concentrated detergent removes grease, oils, and food residues effortlessly while being gentle on hands.

How to Use:
For light cleaning, mix 5-10ml per litre of warm water. For heavy grease, apply undiluted detergent directly to the surface, scrub, and rinse thoroughly with potable water.

Properties:
A refreshing lemon fragrance with a yellow liquid formulation and biodegradable surfactants.

Eco Choice Aotearoa licensed and MPI C32 approved (all animal products except dairy).

Ideal For:
Use in food preparation areas, healthcare, and child-friendly facilities for efficient and safe cleaning.
