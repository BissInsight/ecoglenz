Product Overview:

GREENRHINO™ Enviro Spray & Wipe is a professional-grade, eco-friendly multipurpose cleaner suitable for a wide range of hard surfaces including polished floors, walls, and benches. It effectively removes greasy soils and grime, leaving a fresh, pleasant fragrance.

Key Features:

  • Mild pH formula (6.5 - 7.5) for gentle yet effective cleaning.
  • Meets Environmental Choice New Zealand standards.
  • Approved for use on all animal products except dairy (MPI C32).
  • Designed for professional use – not intended for the general public.

How to Use:

For best results, dilute 1 part cleaner to 4-10 parts water depending on the application. Suitable for spray-and-wipe cleaning of tables, walls, and food preparation surfaces.

Approvals and Certifications

This product is licensed under Eco Choice Aotearoa (Licence no. 5819156) and approved by New Zealand's Ministry for Primary Industries (MPI) under code C32.

Environmental Impact

All detergents affect the environment. Use the recommended dilution to minimize waste and environmental impact.
