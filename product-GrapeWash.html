Grape Wash™ enhances finish and gloss on all paint types. Perfect for hand car washing and maintaining vehicles between waxing. This environmentally friendly car wash solution is biodegradable and certified by Environmental Choice.

Its thick purple liquid formulation, with a neutral pH of 7, makes it safe for all vehicle surfaces. Completely silicone-free and easy to use.

Directions for use:

  • Pour 125ml (1/2 cup) into a clean 20-liter wash bucket and add cold water.
  • Mix to create suds, then hose down the car to soften dirt.
  • Using a wash mitt or sponge, clean the car from the roof down, rinsing regularly.
  • Rinse suds off with low-pressure water and dry with a chamois or towel to prevent spotting.

For best results, work in small sections on hot days to avoid drying suds.
